When you send us your CV.

An automated confirmation e-mail will be sent to you immediately that we received your application.
The resumes we receive are kept at our base for three years for future evaluations and career opportunities.

When we invite you for an interview

We will notify you of the date of the interview and the completion of the professional profile evaluation tools at least 48 hours in advance.

Your interview

In the first interview you are greeted by a specialized representative of the Human Resources Department.

We discuss your resume and professional experiences and analyze the behaviors you have demonstrated in your professional career.

We use psychometric assessment tools that allow us to receive more information about your professional personality and case studies to assess specific skills, behaviors and knowledge related to the needs of the job.

During the interview, we encourage you to ask questions to learn more about the position you are interested in and answer your questions about the work environment and development opportunities at the Sarantis Group.

When we decide on the result

When we make our choice, we inform you of the outcome of the process in time regardless of the outcome of the process (positive / negative response).

We respect your personal data and follow all legal procedures in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Authority (GDPR). The results of your evaluation as well as your CV are strictly confidential.

Traineeship and Apprenticeship Programs

We cooperate with top universities and professional institutions of the country, who actively support the integration of young people into the workplace, for Apprenticeship and Traineeship Programs.

What are we looking for?

Young graduates with high academic performance, a will to learn and evolve and dreams of a successful career course!

We distinguish people with creative attitudes who seek innovation and share our values and culture.

We only accept applications from students who qualify to take an internship, based on their school's curriculum.
